Help

The app uses Leaflet with no-key map tiles. CARTO Voyager is the default basemap, and OpenStreetMap variants are available from the Map tab.

Google Maps output is generated as a directions URL. Route order is preserved, and Google Maps may limit the number of waypoints it accepts.

For many waypoints, the app splits routes into ordered segments. Each segment overlaps the previous endpoint as the next origin.

Straight-line distance is not driving distance. Estimated drive distance and drive time are unavailable unless a routing provider is configured outside this v1 flow.

History is stored only in localStorage on this device and browser. There is no backend, account, sync, analytics, or tracking.

Road-following route mode is experimental and uses the public OSRM driving demo service. If it is blocked or unavailable, the app keeps the straight-line route.
